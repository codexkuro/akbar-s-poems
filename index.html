In the Eclipse

In the eclipse, who are severe and nurture.
Severe in my eye. But you,
Come to save me like a wave.
Calming and throughout.

Such, you are such an artist.
Both in your life and my life.
With your palette, painting my world with full of color.
And it feels, like wake up from an Eclipse.
Like an easel that carried me to fight for the world.
Only for you.

Contagious Light

When there seems like no hope at all.
When i locked myself in the room with a blind dark.
When i fall sometimes and can't stand up.
When my sorrow of tears flooded my room and i'm drowned.

Here comes a light. A dim light.
Contagious... and i felt as if I were being guided.
I'm trying to stand up, trying to stay still.
I feel like this light regenerate my shattered heart.
And finally, I was able to get back on my feet.

Hence i wonder, who is this light?
I thought i had alzheimer.
And I found out it was you.
You are forever my light.
