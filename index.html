In the Eclipse

In the eclipse, who are severe and nurture.
Severe in my eye. But you,
Come to save me like a wave.
Calming and throughout.

Such, you are such an artist.
Both in your life and my life.
With your palette, painting my world with full of color.
And it feels, like wake up from an Eclipse.
Like an easel that carried me to fight for the world.
Only for you.

Wave, a wave.

In the side of a sea, you are like my wave.
You always come and go back to my mind.
In the deep of ocean, my heart drowned.
Just please be a fish, Dear.
Take back my heart to you.

There is only one my favourite fish in the sea, Dear.
Who do you think is it?
That's you, no one else.
I won't change, never.
